# Roadmap

## Master Product Direction

Flim is becoming the decision layer above entertainment services.

North-star question: "What should we watch tonight?"

Long-range direction:

- Spotify playlists for movies and TV shows.
- Movie Night Roulette.
- Watch provider discovery.
- Plex and personal media library awareness.
- Practical playback handoff to supported providers and devices.

See `master-entertainment-roadmap.md` for the full provider, Plex, smart TV, TV series, and advanced Roulette roadmap.

## Phase 1: Personal Movie Lists

Allow users to save movies into poster-first personal playlists with names, descriptions, and watched status.

## Phase 2: Playlist Sharing

Allow playlist links and basic view access for friends and family.

Planning surfaces: private/shared/public visibility, share links, save playlist, and clone playlist.

## Phase 3: Collaboration

Allow invited collaborators to add movies, update statuses, and coordinate shared lists.

Planning surfaces: playlist collaborators, recommended-by attribution, and shared family or friend playlists.

## Phase 4: Discovery

Allow browsing and following public playlists through poster-first shelves and public playlist cards.

Planning surfaces: public playlist discovery, playlist follower counts, and popularity signals.

## Phase 5: Recommendation System

Introduce recommendation logic after enough product behavior and data contracts exist.

Planning surfaces: recommendation badges, recommendation attribution, and future engine output contracts.

## Phase 6: Advanced Social Features

Explore richer social layers only after the core playlist product is stable.

Planning surfaces: playlist popularity, who recommended a movie, discussion/comments, and watch parties.

## Entertainment Platform Expansion

These phases should happen after the core playlist/sharing/database flows are stable:

1. Provider visibility.
2. Provider filtering.
3. Plex library integration.
4. Plex playback.
5. TV show and episode tracking.
6. Advanced smart TV support.
7. Advanced Roulette modes and filters.
